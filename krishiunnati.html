iREAP
Rural entrepreneurship and agribusiness preincubation
The i REAP (Rural Entrepreneurship and Agribusiness Preincubation) is a mentor driven, curated and customized agri incubation program. It aims to identify and promote early stage startups/entrepreneurs in the agri based economy and provide them quality support in terms of mentorship, access to labs, technology credits, funding, industry partnerships and workspaces to ensure their growth as an enterprise.
