Pre-Incubation



Pre-Incubation Program has been started to support early-stage startups in their journey from ideation to seed to growth stage. A total of 31 startups have been selected for the 1st cohort of the program from January 2022 to June 2022. The features of the program include Startup Edu Series, Mentorship from BITS Alumni, Prototype Review, and much more. The facilities being provided would also include research facilities, technical support, mentorship, business connections and the right foundation for a sustainable business.
